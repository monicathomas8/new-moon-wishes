🌙 Luna — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What data we collect

Luna stores your journal entries, wishes and preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Luna will send you moon phase updates. No personal data is collected or shared to do this.

Third parties

Luna does not share any data with third parties.
